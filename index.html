About This Project

This simple yet powerful tool enables you to securely encrypt and decrypt your files directly in your browser. Using strong AES-GCM encryption with a user-defined password, it ensures your sensitive data remains confidential and accessible only to those with the correct key. No data leaves your device, giving you full control over your privacy and security.
